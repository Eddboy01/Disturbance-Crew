Home of Disturbance

Welcome to the home of Chicago's very own Disturbance dance Crew! A group of young talented individuals who love to express ourselves through music.




Who is Disturbance

Welcome to the disturbance website and thank you taking the time to check us out! Disturbance is a chicago based dance crew that specializes in multiple arts such as dance, music and videography. Just a group of young enthusiastic individuals trying to make a name for ourselves. Motivated and driven, the disturbance crew strives to promote excellence and enthusiasm while maintaining a positve image. We perform for most events wheter it be modeling for Clothing brands or coming up with a choreographed routine for your upcoming music video. Thanks in advance for all bookings and support.

Disturbance Content

Disturbance is more than just any ordinary dance group. Our goal is to make sure that all of the content we release to our audience is clean and respectful, while continuing to maintain a postive oveerall image. The majority of our content can be viewed on our youtube page, but feel free to add our other major media outlets for the behind the scene footage.
